T Tumor Size
T1 Tumor < 2 cm
T2 Tumor > 2 to 4 cm
T3 Tumor > 4 cm with or without minimal extra thyroidal extension
T4a Any size, extending beyond capsule to invade subcutaneous tissue, larynx, trachea, esophagus or recurrent laryngeal nerve
T4b Tumor invading pre vertebral fascia or encases carotid artery or mediastinal vessels
Tx Size unknown without extra thyroidal extension
N Lymph Node
N0 No metastatic lymph node
N1a Mets to level V1 (pretracheal, paralaryngeal & prelaryngeal)
N1b Mets to unilateral, bilateral, contralateral cervical or superior mediastinal lymph nodes
Nx Nodes not assessed
M Metastasis
M0 No metastasis
M1 Distant Metastasis
Mx Metastasis not assessed
Stage Age <45 Age 45 or older
Stage I Any T, N but M0 T1, N0, M0
Stage II Any T, N but M1 T2, N0, M0
Stage III T3N0M0, T1N1aM0, T2N1aM0, T3N1aM0
Stage IV A T4aN0M0, T4aN1aM0, T1N1bM0, T2N1bM0, T3N1bM0, T4aN1bMo
Stage IV B T4b any N and M0
Stage IV C Any T or N but M1
